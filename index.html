Extended S&OP (eS&OP) Engine
Concord unifies inter-firm collaboration and intra-firm reconciliation into a single planning workflow. Partner-facing forecast and exception handling follows the industry-standard VICS nine-step CPFR framework, while internal alignment across sales, operations, and finance runs on the standard five-step monthly S&OP cycle — bridged automatically so resolved partner exceptions flow straight into the executive review queue.

This workspace is running in demo mode with sample data so you can explore the full workflow before connecting a live ERP or POS feed. Data entered here is saved locally to this browser.
